# DMES Reference
### Identity Navigation Stack — Workshop 1: Reading the Field

---

## DMES — four coordinates, not four traits

Every Face configuration is defined by four state variables.  
These are not personality dimensions. They are coordinates of a current field activation.  
The same person has different DMES values in different Faces.

| Vector | What it measures | Read it in |
|--------|-----------------|------------|
| **D — Direction** | Gravitational pull of the field. Where does it move toward when narration is unavailable? | Choices under pressure — not declarations. Gap between stated values and revealed preferences. |
| **M — Meaning** | What carries weight. What is numinous enough to elaborate, too costly to approach. | Expansion vs. compression ratio. What the person returns to unprompted. What they pass over quickly. |
| **E — Expression** | How the field presents itself. Register, tone, what it conceals. | Register shifts without apparent cause. Discrepancy between tone and content. |
| **S — State** | Current activation depth. How strongly the current configuration resists change. | Resistance to reframing (high β). Speed of return to dominant narrative after perturbation. |

---

## Three interaction modes

The system selects mode based on current field state (ξ, β).

| Mode | When | What it does |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Narrative** | ξ low — field too stable | Follow movement of thought. No framing, no categories. Let the field move. |
| **Targeted** | ξ > 0.3, β < 0.7 — window open | Single-point intervention. Target the DMES vector with lowest confidence. One precise situation, not a question. |
| **Play** | β > 0.5 or conflict block active | Hypothetical scenario. Reduces narrative defense. The scenario is topographically targeted — not arbitrary. |

**Cartographic Window:** ξ above threshold AND β below ceiling.  
Too stable → field stays closed. Too mobile → field shuts down. Work between.

---

## Face — attractor configuration

A Face is not a personality type.  
It is a recurring configuration that the field returns to after perturbation.

- **Not extracted in one pass.** Crystallises across repeated observations in different blocks.
- **Confirmed** when the same configuration appears in 2+ blocks.
- **Deep** (high β) when the field defends it without noticing the defence.

---

## Completeness criterion (MVP)

The map is ready when all four conditions are met:

| Condition | Check |
|-----------|-------|
| Faces found | Minimum 2 distinct Face configurations identified |
| Face confirmed | At least 1 Face visible across 2 or more blocks |
| Duality satisfied | At least 1 Face has a localised antipode hypothesis |
| DMES partial | All four vectors have confidence > 0.2 |

**Antipode** — the configuration the field moves away from, suppresses, or routes around.  
Not the opposite of a Face. The structural counterforce that defines it.

---

## Five-block architecture

Blocks are phases — they recur, nest, and overlap. Not a linear sequence.

| Block | Signal focus | Mode |
|-------|-------------|------|
| **1 Narrative** | Movement of thought, expansion/compression | Narrative |
| **2 Choices** | Revealed preferences, D-vector under mild pressure | Targeted |
| **3 Conflict** | Topological tension, shadow Face edges, β measurable | Play / Targeted |
| **4 Pressure** | Self-image recedes, field dynamics more direct | Play |
| **5 Future Simulation** | Trajectory, D-vector clearest, absent configurations = data | Play |
